Caesar Salad

What is a Caesar Salad?

A Caesar salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice, olive oil, eggs or egg yolks, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. It was originally prepared tableside.

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp Croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps:

How to Make Caesar Salad Croutons:

  1. Cut baguette in half lengthwise and slice into 1/4â€³ thick pieces and place on baking sheet.
  2. Combine 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil with minced garlic. toss breads with garlic infused oil and 2 Tbsp parmesan.
  3. Spread Evenly and Bake to desired crispness.

How to Make Caesar Salad Dressing:

  1. Whisk together minced garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
  2. Whisking while adding oil emulsifies the dressing for a smooth and creamy (not oily) consistency.
  3. Season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper, or to taste.

How to Make Caesar Salad

In a large mixing bowl, combine all of your ingredients and toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing. This recipe makes enough croutons for two full salads so youll have them ready to go for round 2!
